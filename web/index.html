FAQ

Common questions

How do I find scrap metal prices near me?
Download Scrappalot free from the App Store or Google Play. Allow location access, photograph your metal or enter it manually, and the app instantly shows you ranked payout estimates for every scrap yard within 75 miles.
Which scrap yard pays the most near me?
Scrappalot ranks all nearby yards by how much they'll actually pay for your specific metals — not a generic price. The top-ranked yard is highlighted in green. Yards are sorted by best total payout, not just by distance.
How accurate are the price estimates?
Estimates are based on current commodity market benchmarks, regional multipliers, and each yard's known payout factor. They're directionally accurate — great for comparing yards against each other. Always confirm final price at the yard.
What metals does it recognize?
Copper (all grades), aluminum (cast, sheet, extrusion, radiators), steel, stainless steel, brass, lead, zinc, nickel, and more — including EV battery metals like lithium and cobalt.
Is it really free?
Yes. Scrappalot is free to download and use. GreenChainz is building a sustainable business through premium features and enterprise tools — the core yard comparison and price estimation will always be free for individual users.
What's the GreenChainz Battery Passport?
Scan or enter an EV battery serial number and Scrappalot decodes its chemistry, manufacture era, and precious metal content — giving you a far more accurate payout estimate than generic "battery scrap" prices.
